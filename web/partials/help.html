Match Predictions

  • Every match contains 5 parameters, each successful prediction is 2 points
    • Who will win?
    • How many goals team 1 will score?
    • How many goals team 2 will score?
    • How many goal difference will be at full time?
    • Who will score first?
  • Submit prediction until one hour before kick off time

Team Predictions

  • Every prediction has different credit according to the type
    • Champion is 16 points
    • Runner up is 8 points
    • Each group winner or runner up is 4 points
    • Each 3rd place is 4 points
  • Submit prediction until one hour before deadline time

Simulator

  • Once the deadline to fill a prediction is over, it will be available at the simulator's page to see your position according to real time game score.
